The Truman Show - Full Review

Directed by: Peter Weir

Starring: Jim Carrey, Laura Linney, Ed Harris

Genre: Drama, Sci-Fi

Released: 1998

The Truman Show is a groundbreaking film that explores the concept of reality television and privacy. Jim Carrey plays Truman Burbank, a man who unknowingly lives his life in a TV show, with every aspect of his world carefully orchestrated. The film offers an insightful commentary on the nature of reality, free will, and the manipulation of human emotions. Carrey’s performance is brilliantly layered, showcasing his versatility beyond comedy. The Truman Show remains relevant in today’s age of social media and surveillance culture.

Rating from CineBox:

8.5/10

A thought-provoking movie that remains timeless and continues to resonate with modern societal issues about surveillance and identity.
