Dead Poets Society - Full Review

Directed by: Peter Weir

Starring: Robin Williams, Ethan Hawke, Robert Sean Leonard

Genre: Drama

Released: 1989

Dead Poets Society is an iconic film that highlights the power of education and the importance of thinking for oneself. Robin Williams plays John Keating, an inspiring teacher who challenges his students to question the status quo and embrace the world of literature. The movie is an emotional journey about the struggles of adolescence, the pressure of societal expectations, and the beauty of self-expression. Robin Williams’ performance is unforgettable, making this a must-watch for anyone seeking inspiration.

Rating from CineBox:

9.5/10

A truly moving and life-affirming film, thanks to its powerful performances and timeless message about education and individuality.
