Detachment - Full Review

Directed by: Tony Kaye

Starring: Adrien Brody, Marcia Gay Harden, James Caan

Genre: Drama

Released: 2011

Detachment is a raw and emotionally charged film about a substitute teacher, played by Adrien Brody, who forms connections with his troubled students while grappling with his own personal struggles. The film offers an unflinching portrayal of the education system and the challenges teachers face. It is an intense watch, but the stellar performances, particularly from Brody, elevate the movie. The film explores isolation, hope, and the impact of education on both students and teachers alike.

Rating from CineBox:

8/10

An emotional and thought-provoking film that provides a deep look into the struggles of both teachers and students.
